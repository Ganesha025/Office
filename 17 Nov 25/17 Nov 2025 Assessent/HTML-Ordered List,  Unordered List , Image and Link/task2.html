Baashha (1995) - Movie Review

"Baashha," directed by Suresh Krissna, is an iconic Tamil action drama starring Superstar Rajinikanth. The film follows Manickam, a humble auto driver with a mysterious and violent past. As the story unfolds, his hidden identity as the feared underworld don "Manick Baashha" resurfaces, revealing a gripping tale of power, loyalty, and redemption. Known for its unforgettable dialogues, powerful performances, and mass appeal, "Baashha" remains one of the most celebrated films in Tamil cinema.

Main Cast

  2. Nagma as Priya
